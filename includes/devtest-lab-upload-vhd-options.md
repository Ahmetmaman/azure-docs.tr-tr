## <a name="prerequisites"></a>Ön koşullar

- [AzCopy komut satırı yardımcı programını kullanarak yükleme](../articles/lab-services/devtest-lab-upload-vhd-using-azcopy.md)
- [Microsoft Azure Depolama Gezgini'ni kullanarak yükleme](../articles/lab-services/devtest-lab-upload-vhd-using-storage-explorer.md)
- [PowerShell konsolu kullanarak yükleme](../articles/lab-services/devtest-lab-upload-vhd-using-powershell.md)
